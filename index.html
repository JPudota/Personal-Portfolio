About me

I am an experienced IT professional with over eight years of expertise as an Oracle Utilities Application Administrator, specializing in systems like CC&B, OUCSS, OUMDM, WebLogic, and Oracle Database 12c.
After relocating to New Zealand and taking a career break to raise my family, I embraced the opportunity to expand my expertise by joining a Data Analyst internship program with Industry Connect and MVP Studio.

There, I mastered modern data analytics tools, including Microsoft SQL Server, Power BI, SSIS, SSRS, and Tableau, building dynamic dashboards and robust ETL processes to deliver data-driven insights.

With a passion for problem-solving and a dedication to embracing cutting-edge technologies, I'm always eager to learn, explore new tools, and contribute to meaningful, data-driven solutions.
